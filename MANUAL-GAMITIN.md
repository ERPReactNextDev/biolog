# BIOLOG USER MANUAL

## How to Use the Biolog System

### What is Biolog?

Biolog is a system for managing users and their information. This system provides the ability to login, manage profiles, and perform various functions depending on your role.

---

## COMMON ROLES

### 1. **Test Biolog** (Example Role)
- Can login to the system
- Can view own profile
- Can update personal information

---

## HOW TO LOGIN

1. **Open the Biolog application**
   - Go to the Biolog website
   - Or open the mobile app (if available)

2. **Enter your credentials**
   - **Email**: `t.biolog@buildchemsolutions.com` (or your registered email)
   - **Password**: Your personal password

3. **Click the "Login" button**
   - Wait a few seconds for your account to be verified

4. **If login is successful**
   - You will be directed to the dashboard or home page
   - You will see your profile and available features

---

## SYSTEM FEATURES

### 1. **Profile Management**
- View your own profile
- Update personal information
- Upload profile picture
- Update signature image

### 2. **Login Security**
- Password protection
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) - optional
- Face verification - optional
- Login attempts tracking
- Account lock protection

### 3. **Account Status**
- **Active**: Can login and use the system
- **Locked**: Cannot login (if too many failed attempts)
- **Inactive**: Account is no longer active

---

## HOW TO UPDATE PROFILE

1. **Go to Profile page**
   - Click "Profile" in the menu
   - Or click your profile picture

2. **Edit the fields**
   - Click the field you want to update
   - Enter the new information
   - Click "Save" or "Update"

3. **If update is successful**
   - You will see a notification "Profile updated successfully"
   - The new information is saved

---

## SECURITY FEATURES

### Password
- Your password should be secure
- Do not share it with others
- Change your password regularly

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Optional feature
- Provides an extra layer of security
- Requires OTP code after password

### Face Verification
- Optional feature
- Uses face recognition
- More secure login method

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Cannot login?
1. Check your email and password
2. Check if your account is active
3. Check if your account is locked (contact admin)
4. Reset your password if forgotten

### Cannot update profile?
1. Check if there are required fields not filled
2. Check your internet connection
3. Refresh the page and try again

### Forgot password?
1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Follow the instructions in the email
